Services we provide

House Loan

Housing loan is a secured financial product offered by banks and housing finance companies to assist individuals in purchasing, constructing, or renovating residential properties. In this arrangement, the property itself serves as collateral until the loan is fully repaid .Borrower has to repay the amount along with the interest.

Personal Loan

Personal loan is a type of unsecured loan provided by financial institutions such as banks, credit unions, or online lenders. Unlike secured loans, personal loans do not require collateral, meaning you don't need to pledge assets like your home or car to obtain the loan.

Business Loan

Business loan is a financial product designed to provide capital to businesses for various operational needs, such as expansion, inventory purchase, equipment acquisition, or managing cash flow. These loans are typically repaid over a predetermined period, with interest, through regular installments.

Loan Against Property

Loan against property (LAP) is a secured loan where borrowers can pledge their residential or commercial property as collateral to obtain funds. This type of loan is typically used for large expenses such as business expansion, education, or medical emergencies. The loan amount is usually a percentage of the property's market value, and borrowers benefit from lower interest rates compared to unsecured loans.
